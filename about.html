Grounded, consent-led healing

I founded Release to Rise Healing to give people a calm, trustworthy space to let go of what’s heavy and reconnect to balance—mentally, emotionally, and spiritually. My approach blends Reiki, sound healing, and gentle breathwork with a trauma-informed, consent-led framework. Sessions are collaborative and paced to your comfort, with simple aftercare so benefits carry into daily life.

Training & credentials

  • Certified Reiki Master
  • Certified Sound Healer
  • Breathwork practitioner
  • Trauma-informed practice; hands-off by default

What a session feels like

Most clients describe sessions as deeply relaxing, emotionally clarifying, and physically grounding. We start with a brief check‑in, set intentions, and then move into energy work (typically non‑touch). You can expect a warm, quiet environment, optional soothing sound elements, and a short integration chat afterward with practical suggestions.

Who I work with

People navigating stress, burnout, life transitions, grief, or a general sense of “heaviness.” Many are seeking a complementary modality alongside therapy or medical care. Reiki and energy work are gentle and supportive—designed to walk with you, not replace professional medical or mental health care.

Values

  • Safety & consent: hands‑off by default; you’re in control of pace and proximity.
  • Clarity & care: plain language, realistic outcomes, practical aftercare.
  • Integrity: grounded, humble, client‑centered work.

FAQ

Is this a massage?

No. This is energy-based bodywork and is not a clinical massage. Sessions are hands‑off by default and tailored to your comfort.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothing you can relax in. You’ll remain fully clothed throughout the session.

How often should I book?

Many clients start with 2–3 sessions over a month, then move to monthly or as‑needed for maintenance. We’ll adjust based on your goals.

Note: Energy work is a complementary modality and not a substitute for licensed medical or mental health care.
